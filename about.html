About This Project

More Good Work Talk is an s12k Work Play project. It's a way to get better at speaking impromptu at work leveraging different mental frameworks. It's meant to be simple, useful, and load quickly.

Eventually More Good Work Talk might get expanded in different ways: new mental models (STAR would be a good one) - maybe different topic lists to choose from (Product vs Eng vs Art vs Parenting?)
