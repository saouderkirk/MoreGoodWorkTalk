How to Use More Good Work Talk

  1. Click "Start" to begin a session and receive a topic.
  2. Speak for the given time, following the structured prompts.
  3. Review your recording to assess clarity, conciseness, and impact.
  4. Click "Another?" to start a new session with a fresh topic.
  5. Download your recordings for personal review or feedback from others.
